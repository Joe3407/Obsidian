- **Periodic Motion:**
	- Motion that repeats itself after equal intervals of time, (Fan, Earth Orbit)
	- **Types:**
		- **Oscillatory Motion:**
			- Motion where body moves forward and backwards about a fixed equilibrium position, and repeats in time, (Spring, Pendulum)
			- **Types:**
				- **Simple Harmonic Motion(SMH):**
					- Only when $F\propto x$ 
					- Types:
						- Spring
						- Simple Pendulum
						- Compound Pendulum 
- **Spring:**
	- **Hook's Law:** $F=-kx$, (Restoring Force)
	- $(F=ma) \to (-kx=ma) \to (a=-\frac{k}{m}x) =(\frac{d^2x}{dt^2})$ 
	- $(w^2=\frac{k}{m}) \to (a=-w^2x)$        $(w=2\pi f)\to (w=\frac{2\pi}{T})\to (T=\frac{2\pi}{w})$   
	- $x(t)=Acos(wt+\phi)$

	- $a(t)=-w^2Acos(wt+\phi)$

		- $(E=K.E+U_s) \to (E=\frac{1}{2}mv^2+\frac{1}{2}kx^2)\to (\frac{1}{2}m\frac{k}{m}A^2sin^2(wt+\phi)+\frac{1}{2}kA^2cos^2(wt+\phi))\to (\frac{1}{2}kA^2(sin^2(wt+\phi)+cos^2(wt+\phi)))\to(\frac{1}{2}kA^2)$
